MSCS, University of Rochester (Rochester, NY, USA) (Present)

I work on domain shift and representation learning , focusing on recovering latent signals under distribution shift. I am particularly interested in continual learning.
While my work spans medical imaging, my core focus is building robust deep learning models for real-world, noisy data.

Research

I design and implement deep learning models for learning from indirect and weakly supervised signals under real-world data variability.

I completed graduate coursework in Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning at Korea University with a perfect GPA (4.5/4.5). My implementations and related resources are available on GitHub or upon request.
